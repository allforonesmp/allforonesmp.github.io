SMP Rules

Promoting fair gameplay, structured punishments, and an automated jail system.
Contact staff if any action appears unjust.

Not agreeing to all the rules on the server can result in a PERMANENT BAN

The rules are subject to modification or expansion to serve the community's needs. In case of disputes, matters will be resolved through community voting.

Players are encouraged to record gameplay using Windows Game Bar or ScreenRec to provide evidence for resolving disputes.

Administrative Authority

  • Admins may remove items suspected to be stolen, duplicated, or obtained through exploits.
  • Admins can remove unauthorized builds; returned materials depend on community consensus.
  • Financial penalties may be applied as an alternative to jailing.
  • Admins may inspect chests for illegal or duplicated items.
  • Jail times may be assigned according to the standard guidelines below.
  • Admins may spectate players to monitor gameplay for compliance.
Admins are prohibited from exploiting knowledge of hidden areas or resources discovered during spectating, except for monitoring purposes or with explicit player consent.

Community Conduct

  • Maintain respect for all players.
  • No bullying, harassment, or targeted negative behavior.
  • Keep chat clean and free from spam or offensive content.
  • Swearing in chat or proximity communication is not permitted.

Punishments:

Infractions may result in jail times ranging from 2 to 10 hours, as determined by community guidelines.

Gameplay Regulations

  • No griefing or theft from other players.
  • PvP is only allowed when all participants consent.
  • No stalking or unauthorized access to player bases.
  • Respect farms, chests, and shops; take only when permitted.
  • Greifing and sealing is ONLY allowed if the victim's land is unclaimed

Punishments:

Violations may result in jail times between 5 and 20 hours, depending on the severity and community feedback.

Building Guidelines

  • Request permission before building near another player's base.
  • Clean up after resource gathering or construction.
  • Maintain communal areas in a safe and orderly manner.
  • Clearly label shared or public constructions.

Punishments:

Violations may result in jail times between 2 and 5 hours, as determined by community consensus.

Economy & Trading

  • All trades must be transparent and mutually agreed upon.
  • Restock public farms after use.
  • Shops must display accurate pricing.

Punishments:

Violations may result in jail times from 2 to 20 hours for serious offenses such as theft.

Technical Restrictions

  • Use of hacked clients, exploits, or X-ray is prohibited.
  • Avoid creating lag-inducing machines or excessive redstone circuits.
  • Mob farms must be safe for all players nearby.
  • Redstone contraptions must include an accessible off switch.
  • Large farms (50+ blocks) require admin approval; otherwise, they may be removed without restitution.
  • Avoid dumping large quantities of items on the ground to prevent server lag.
  • Any designs/conraptions/afk farms/redstone machines that run more than ONCE every 20 ticks MUST require admin approval

Punishments:

Violations may result in jail times ranging from 6 to 15 hours, particularly if server stability is affected.

PvP & Conflict

  • PvP requires explicit agreement between all players involved.
  • No revenge cycles or harassment following combat.
  • No killing intended solely to provoke or annoy other players.
  • Owners may attack trespassers on their land (If the trespasser Griefs or steals)

Punishments:

Violations may result in jail times between 1 and 30 hours, especially if items are destroyed or murder has occured.
